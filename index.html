WHOAMI

 

 

Personal

My full name is Dileep Venkatarama Reddy. My HAM-radio call sign is KI7HDF. I hail from Bangalore, a city in the southern part of India. I earned a bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering and a Master's degree in Communications from the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras (IITM), located in another southern city in India. Soon after, I jumped ship and landed a doctoral degree from the Physics Department at the University of Oregon in Eugene, a town in western USA.

I currently work with my advisor Prof. Michael G. Raymer at Raymer Lab, where I do some Quantum Optics theory, and solder leads, turn knobs and align laser-beams in a cold neon basement lab.
